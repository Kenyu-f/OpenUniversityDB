# Caltech — Research Centers

`universities/caltech/research_centers.md`

> Caltech states it is home to **more than 50 research centers and institutes**. This file covers the major cross-divisional centers/institutes identified from official sources. It is **not a complete enumeration of all 50+** — coverage should be expanded over time, prioritizing centers most relevant to each division as those divisions are built out. Entries are grouped by primary sponsoring division/office where identifiable; many centers are explicitly interdisciplinary and span multiple divisions.

---

## Institute-Wide / Multi-Divisional Centers

### Beckman Institute
- **Mission:** Multi-disciplinary center advancing research in chemical and biological sciences through shared instrumentation and cross-lab collaboration.
- **Research Topics:** Advanced imaging (biological/cryogenic electron microscopy), optogenetics, genomics, proteomics, molecular materials, laser-based research tools.
- **Participating Departments:** Spans Biology and Biological Engineering, Chemistry and Chemical Engineering, and affiliated programs; houses 15+ constituent facilities/centers (e.g., CLOVER, Functional Genomics Resource Center, Molecular Observatory).
- **Director:** Dennis A. Dougherty (per available sourcing — verify current directorship).
- **Official Website:** [beckmaninstitute.caltech.edu](http://beckmaninstitute.caltech.edu/)

### Resnick Sustainability Institute
- **Mission:** Caltech's hub for sustainability research, combining fundamental science with applied solutions to energy and sustainability challenges.
- **Research Topics:** Renewable energy, sustainability science, energy storage and conversion.
- **Participating Departments:** Cross-divisional (Chemistry and Chemical Engineering, Engineering and Applied Science, Geological and Planetary Sciences).
- **Director:** ⚠️ Not confirmed in sources consulted — verify officially.
- **Official Website:** [resnick.caltech.edu](https://resnick.caltech.edu/)

### Joint Center for Artificial Photosynthesis (JCAP)
- **Mission:** Described as the nation's largest research program dedicated to developing artificial solar-fuel generation technology.
- **Research Topics:** Solar fuels, photoelectrochemistry, catalysis.
- **Participating Departments:** Chemistry and Chemical Engineering; partnered with Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory.
- **Director:** ⚠️ Verify officially.
- **Official Website:** [solarfuelshub.org](https://solarfuelshub.org/) (external JCAP consortium site — verify current status/redirect)

### Kavli Nanoscience Institute (KNI)
- **Mission:** Cross-disciplinary hub for nanoscience and nanotechnology research and shared cleanroom/fabrication facilities.
- **Research Topics:** Nanofabrication, quantum devices, photonics, nanoscale materials.
- **Participating Departments:** Physics, Mathematics and Astronomy; Engineering and Applied Science; Chemistry and Chemical Engineering.
- **Director:** ⚠️ Verify officially.
- **Official Website:** [kni.caltech.edu](https://www.kni.caltech.edu/)

### Keck Institute for Space Studies (KISS)
- **Mission:** A "think and do tank" convening scientists and engineers to develop new space mission concepts and technologies.
- **Founded:** January 2008, with a $24 million grant over 8 years from the W. M. Keck Foundation.
- **Research Topics:** Space mission concept development, cross-disciplinary space technology.
- **Participating Departments:** PMA, EAS, GPS; operates in close partnership with JPL.
- **Director:** ⚠️ Verify officially.
- **Official Website:** [kiss.caltech.edu](https://kiss.caltech.edu/)

### Center for Autonomous Systems and Technologies (CAST)
- **Mission:** Advances research in autonomous systems (aerial, ground, and other robotic platforms).
- **Research Topics:** Autonomy, robotics, control systems, drones.
- **Participating Departments:** Engineering and Applied Science.
- **Director:** ⚠️ Verify officially.
- **Official Website:** [cast.caltech.edu](https://www.cast.caltech.edu/)

### American Institute of Mathematics (AIM)
- **Mission:** Supports mathematical research through focused workshops and collaboration (an NSF-funded math institute affiliated with Caltech).
- **Research Topics:** Pure and applied mathematics, broad topic areas set by workshop themes.
- **Participating Departments:** Mathematics (PMA).
- **Director:** ⚠️ Verify officially.
- **Official Website:** [aimath.org](https://aimath.org/)

## Physics, Mathematics and Astronomy (PMA) Centers

### Institute for Quantum Information and Matter (IQIM)
- **Mission:** An NSF **Physics Frontiers Center** exploiting and advancing the emerging revolution in quantum science.
- **Research Topics:** Quantum information, quantum matter, quantum computing foundations.
- **Participating Departments:** Physics; cross-listed with Applied Physics and Materials Science (EAS).
- **Director:** ⚠️ Verify officially.
- **Official Website:** [iqim.caltech.edu](https://iqim.caltech.edu/)

### Infrared Processing and Analysis Center (IPAC)
- **Mission:** Partners with NASA, NSF, JPL, and the global research community to advance exploration of the universe through science operations, data archiving, and community engagement.
- **Research Topics:** Infrared astronomy, astronomical data archiving, mission science operations (e.g., Spitzer, WISE/NEOWISE legacy archives).
- **Participating Departments:** Astronomy (PMA); operated in partnership with JPL/NASA.
- **Director:** ⚠️ Verify officially.
- **Official Website:** [ipac.caltech.edu](https://www.ipac.caltech.edu/)

### Walter Burke Institute for Theoretical Physics
- **Mission:** Endowed research center supporting Caltech's theoretical physics community.
- **Research Topics:** Fundamental laws of nature, theoretical physics across scales.
- **Participating Departments:** Physics (PMA).
- **Director:** ⚠️ Verify officially.
- **Official Website:** [theory.caltech.edu](https://theory.caltech.edu/)

## NASA-Affiliated / Managed Facility

### Jet Propulsion Laboratory (JPL)
- **Mission:** Federally funded research and development center and NASA field center; constructs and operates robotic planetary spacecraft, conducts Earth-orbit and astronomy missions, and operates NASA's Deep Space Network.
- **Research Topics:** Planetary exploration, robotics, deep-space communications, Earth science.
- **Participating Departments:** Managed by Caltech for NASA; draws collaboration across all divisions.
- **Director:** ⚠️ Verify current JPL Director officially (this is a senior, frequently-updated leadership position).
