# Caltech — Alumni

`universities/caltech/alumni.md`

> **A note on conflicting counts:** Sources consulted gave inconsistent Nobel-laureate figures — Caltech's own undergraduate admissions page states **"17 Nobel Laureates"** among living alumni; Wikipedia (citing more recent, broader "alumni + faculty" methodology) states **30 alumni and 16 non-alumni faculty (46 total)** have won the Nobel Prize as of October 2022; other secondary sites cite yet other totals (19, 48). These numbers differ because they use **different populations** (living alumni only vs. all-time alumni vs. alumni+faculty+postdocs combined). **Use Caltech's own [official Nobel Laureates page](https://www.caltech.edu/about/legacy/awards-and-honors/nobel-laureates) as the authoritative source**, and always specify which population a cited figure refers to.

## 2. Turing Award Winners (Alumni)

- Caltech states that **six alumni** have won the **ACM Turing Award** ("the Nobel Prize of Computer Science"), as of the most recent Wikipedia-sourced count (Oct. 2022).
- **Ivan Sutherland** — pioneering work in interactive computer graphics; Turing Award (1988); also won the Kyoto Prize in Advanced Technology (2012).
- ⚠️ The remaining five Turing laureates were not individually confirmed by name in sources consulted for this pass — verify the complete list via the [ACM Turing Award site](https://amturing.acm.org) cross-referenced with Caltech affiliation.

## 3. Fields Medal

- **Stanislav Smirnov** — Caltech alumnus; awarded the **Fields Medal in 2010** for work in conformal field theory and percolation theory (probability/statistical mechanics-adjacent mathematics).
- As of available sourcing, Smirnov appears to be Caltech's **sole** Fields Medal-winning alumnus.
